中文:路径禁区 Path_Prohibition 定义与核心推导
固有性本质：路径禁区并非光子与障碍物或其他光子作用的产物，而是光子群体 "等距面心排布" 规则的天然盲区 —— 任何违反最小能量间距的空间点，自始至终都不会有光子进入。
实验佐证：结合阿夫沙尔实验的单光子落点统计可直接验证：即使单光子逐个通过狭缝，其落点也会精准避开理论禁区，说明路径选择是光子的内禀属性，而非群体干涉的结果。
路径禁区的放大或缩小，可通过光学工具实现，遵循光的普遍折射定律。比如用放大镜这类工具，会使亮纹间距缩小、禁区范围同步收窄；而通过凹透镜或调整观测光路，可使亮纹间距增大、禁区范围相应拓宽。整个过程中，光子排布的核心规则不被打破，仅通过光路弯折实现宏观尺度上的缩放。
当用光学工具（比如放大镜）作用于光路时，根据光的折射定律，透镜会改变光的传播方向，但不会改变光程差的本质关系。假设原狭缝宽度为 a，禁区范围对应亮纹间距 Δy；经透镜缩放后，等效狭缝宽度变为 a′=k⋅a（k 为缩放系数，k>1 为放大，k<1 为缩小）。
由于光程差 δ 与狭缝宽度、衍射角的关系不变，缩放后的亮纹间距 Δy′=k⋅Δy，即禁区范围随缩放系数同步放大或缩小。这就从光程差的核心逻辑，推导出了路径禁区通过光学工具缩放的合理性。
Engish： Definition and Core Derivation of Path_Prohibition
Intrinsic essence: The path prohibition zone is not produced by the interaction between photons and obstacles or other photons. Instead, it is a natural blind zone formed by the rule of "equidistant face-centered arrangement" of photon groups. Any spatial point that violates the minimum energy spacing will never be entered by photons.Experimental evidence: The single-photon landing statistics of the Afshar experiment can provide direct verification. Even when single photons pass through the slit one by one, their landing positions accurately avoid the theoretical forbidden areas. This indicates that path selection is an intrinsic property of photons, rather than a consequence of group interference.The amplification or reduction of the path prohibition zone can be realized through optical tools and follows the general refraction law of light.Tools such as magnifying glasses can narrow the spacing of bright fringes and synchronously shrink the range of forbidden zones; concave lenses or adjusted optical paths can widen the bright fringe spacing and expand the corresponding forbidden areas.Throughout the process, the core rules of photon arrangement remain unchanged, and macroscopic scaling is only achieved through the bending of the optical path.When optical tools such as magnifying glasses act on the light path, lenses change the propagation direction of light in accordance with the law of refraction, without altering the essential relationship of optical path difference.Assume the original slit width is a, and the forbidden zone range corresponds to the bright fringe spacing Δy. After lens scaling, the equivalent slit width becomes a′=k⋅a (k represents the scaling coefficient; k>1 for amplification and k<1 for reduction).Since the relationship between optical path difference δ, slit width and diffraction angle remains constant, the scaled bright fringe spacing is Δy′=k⋅Δy. Namely, the range of the path prohibition zone expands or shrinks synchronously with the scaling coefficient.Based on the core logic of optical path difference, this derivation confirms the rationality of scaling the path prohibition zone by means of optical tools.
